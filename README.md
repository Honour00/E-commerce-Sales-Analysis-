### Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive sales analysis of an e-commerce dataset covering **2023–2025**.  
The objective of the analysis is to explore revenue patterns, customer behavior, geographic performance, and payment preferences while transforming raw transactional data into actionable insights using **Power BI**.

The analysis focuses on identifying trends, diagnosing performance challenges, and generating data-driven recommendations for business growth.

## Business Questions

The analysis aims to answer the following key questions:

- How has revenue and transaction volume changed over time?
- Which customer segments contribute the most to revenue?
- Which countries drive overall sales performance?
- How do payment preferences influence transactions?
- Where are the biggest opportunities for growth?

## Dataset

The dataset represents transactional e-commerce data and includes attributes such as:

- Revenue / Sales Value  
- Transactions / Orders  
- Customer Demographics  
- Age Groups  
- Country / Geographic Data  
- Payment Methods  

**Note:** The most recent year contains partial data, which affects year-over-year comparisons.

The dataset can be found in the **data folder** of this repository.

### Key Insights

- Revenue and transaction volumes declined year-over-year  
- The decline is partly due to incomplete data for the most recent year  
- **Average Order Value (AOV)** remained strong despite reduced transactions  
- Customers aged **45–54** and **55+** generated the highest revenue share  
- Sales performance is concentrated in a limited number of countries  
- Payment method usage is relatively balanced across available options  

### Business Implications

The findings suggest that declining performance may be linked to:

- Customer engagement and retention challenges  
- Market concentration risks  
- Untapped demographic segments  
- Opportunities for targeted marketing strategies  

### Recommendations

#### Revenue & Customers
- Prioritize retention strategies for high-value customers  
- Implement targeted campaigns for dominant revenue segments  
- Develop acquisition strategies for younger demographics  
- Avoid blanket discounting to maintain strong Average Order Value  

#### Geography & Payments
- Increase marketing focus on high-performing regions  
- Test localized campaigns in emerging markets  
- Monitor payment behavior to improve checkout experience
